About me

Dr. Gu is an Economist specializing in data-driven econometric modeling of market competition and industrial policy. Previously, Dr. Gu served as an Economist and Data Scientist in internet companies such as TikTok, Meituan and Kuaishou. He has also worked as an Assistant Professor and thesis advisor at Peking University HSBC Business School from 2017 to 2021. His publications have been seen on Journal of Financial Econometrics, Energy Economics, Journal of Cleaner Production, etc.
